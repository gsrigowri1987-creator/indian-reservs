About Reservoir Quest

Reservoir Quest is a school wall-magazine project reimagined as an interactive game platform. Instead of reading a poster, you get to play quizzes, puzzles and map challenges built around real reservoirs of South India — their rivers, states, and the stories behind them.

👨‍🎓 About the Creator

Reservoir Quest was created by G. Abidarman, a student of Class X-B at L. M. Dadha Senior Secondary School, Choolaimedu, Chennai.

This project was developed as an educational initiative to make learning about the reservoirs of South India more engaging, interactive, and enjoyable. By combining maps, quizzes, puzzles, photographs, and interesting facts, Reservoir Quest encourages students to explore geography through hands-on learning.

Every section of the website has been carefully designed to provide an enjoyable experience while helping students understand the importance of reservoirs, dams, rivers, water conservation, and the vital role they play in everyday life.

Thank you for exploring Reservoir Quest. I hope this project inspires curiosity, learning, and a greater appreciation for our natural water resources.

— G. Abidarman
Class X-B
L. M. Dadha Senior Secondary School
Choolaimedu, Chennai
